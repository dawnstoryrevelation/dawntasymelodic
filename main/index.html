About Me

Hi, I'm Jasper. At 13, I'm deeply passionate about the fusion of music, artificial intelligence, and writing. My project, Dawntasy Creations, is where I bring these worlds together to make a difference.

In writing, I've published the fantasy-dystopian novella series Dawntasy, beginning with Time's True Name. Musically, you might enjoy my latest Spotify singles like "Constellations" or "Reflection."

My core focus in AI involves exploring advanced concepts like Wasserstein GANs and developing innovative research, including proposals like DDTN (Deep Descriptive Transformative Network). This network introduces a novel matrix-based factorization approach for compact, activated "Descriptors."

Inspired by trailblazers who shape our future, my goal: The world reimagined. From music. From AI. From writing.

Spotlight: DDTN v4.3

DDTN v4.3 (“Networks with Descriptive Projections”) is a revolutionary 1B-parameter LLM architecture, provably rivaling 30B dense Transformers with >80x inference and >60x training speedup. Built on entirely novel modules, it redefines AI efficiency and performance.

  • Unmatched Speed: >80x faster inference, >60x faster training vs. 30B Transformer.
  • Extreme Efficiency: ~7.6x FLOP reduction; 10B token corpus yields "100B-equivalent" signal.
  • Novel Architecture: Hyperbolic Descriptors, USRLR, FleX-Routing.
  • Provably Stable & Robust: <0.01% NaN/crash; >95% adversarial test coverage.
  • Transcendent Intelligence: 1B model MMLU ≥ 70%, matching 30B reference.
  • Radically Simple: ~3200 LOC, ≤ 5 core components.
